Reviewer asked for revision?

First Reaction 02 / 07

Many students think:

“是不是我英文太差？”

But when reviewers say unclear, they may be questioning your logic, evidence, method, or positioning.

English?

Grammar, word choice, sentence clarity.

Argument?

Novelty, method, evidence, contribution.

Do not polish before you diagnose.

Revision is a strategy problem first @yourhandle
4-Part Diagnosis 03 / 07

Classify the comment first.

先判断：审稿人真正质疑什么？

Novelty

What is new compared with prior work?

Method

Can readers understand and trust how you did it?

Data

Is the evidence sufficient for your claim?

Writing

Is the argument easy to follow sentence by sentence?

One reviewer comment can hide different problems.

Novelty / Method / Data / Writing @yourhandle
Novelty 04 / 07

If the issue is novelty...

不要只说“我们强调了创新性”。

Weak reply
Thank you. We have emphasized the novelty of our study.
Stronger reply
We clarified how our study differs from prior work by adding a comparison with X, Y, and Z in the final paragraph of the Introduction.
说明前人做了什么、没做什么、你补了什么。
Do not claim novelty. Position it. @yourhandle
Method & Data 05 / 07

If the issue is method or data...

回应要落到正文修改，而不是态度表态。

Method

Add steps, parameters, sample source, evaluation metrics, or implementation details.

Data

Add statistical results, robustness checks, extra experiments, or explain why current evidence is sufficient.

We added details on sample selection and model parameters in Section 2.3.
Specific revision beats polite wording @yourhandle
Writing 06 / 07

If it really is a writing issue...

也不要只做语法润色。

1.Does each paragraph have a clear topic sentence?
2.Do sentences connect logically?
3.Is the contribution stated explicitly?
Weak
This study is important.
Better
This study contributes to X by showing Y under Z conditions.
Clarity is structure, not only grammar @yourhandle
Checklist 07 / 07

Before you revise, check these 4 things.

返修前，先做这个 4 步诊断。

Is this comment about novelty, method, data, or writing?
Did I revise the manuscript, not just the response letter?
Did I mention the exact section, paragraph, or line?
Did I explain why this revision solves the concern?
